About EverFriends

We're on a mission to ensure no one ever feels alone again, using the power of AI to create meaningful connections and compassionate care for seniors and their families.

Our Story

EverFriends was born from a deeply personal experience. Our founder, Daniel Hungerford, watched his own grandmother struggle with loneliness and isolation as she aged. Despite the best efforts of family members, there were simply not enough hours in the day to provide the constant companionship and support she needed.

This experience led to a realization: millions of seniors face the same challenge every day. They're not just dealing with physical health issues, but with the emotional toll of loneliness, which can be just as devastating. Traditional solutions weren't working – human caregivers can't be available 24/7, and existing technology felt cold and impersonal.

So we set out to create something different. EverFriends isn't just another AI chatbot – it's a digital companion designed with empathy, understanding, and genuine care. We've built our technology to not just respond, but to truly connect with seniors on an emotional level.

Our Mission

To eliminate loneliness and isolation among seniors by providing compassionate, AI-powered digital companions that offer genuine emotional support, meaningful conversation, and practical assistance.

We believe that every senior deserves to feel valued, heard, and connected, regardless of their circumstances or the availability of human caregivers.

Our Vision

A world where no senior ever feels alone, where technology serves humanity with warmth and compassion, and where families can have peace of mind knowing their loved ones are cared for.

We envision a future where AI companions are not just tools, but trusted friends who understand, remember, and genuinely care about the people they serve.
